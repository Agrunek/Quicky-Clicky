# Contributing to Quicky-Clicky

This is my personal mini-project, and I won’t be accepting any code contributions unless I ask for them directly.

## So, what can I do?

If you have any ideas for changes, use the [Issues tab](https://github.com/Agrunek/Quicky-Clicky/issues) and share your thoughts there. If I find your suggestion useful, I will contact you with a follow up.
